About Boba

Boba, or bubble tea, is a Taiwan-orginated sweet beverage created in the 1980's, typically bleding tea,milk and chewy tapioca pearls made from cassava root. known for its high customization, this popular drink can be served hot or cold with various toppings, though it is often high in sugar and calories.

Boba has a sweet, carmel-like flavor, while the tea base can range from earthy (matcha) to floral (jasmine) or creamy(classic milk tea) notes
